About FitAi

Our mission, values, and the passionate team behind the technology that's transforming fitness journeys worldwide

Our Story

FitAi was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate students from CMR Institute of Technology who specialize in LLM & ML technologies. We recognized that personalized fitness guidance should be accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford expensive personal trainers.

"How can we make expert fitness guidance available to everyone?"

This simple question sparked our journey

The answer was artificial intelligence that could analyze individual needs and goals to create truly personalized fitness experiences. Today, FitAi combines scientific research in exercise physiology with cutting-edge AI to create adaptive fitness plans that evolve as you progress.

Thousands Transformed

We're proud to have helped people reach goals they once thought impossible
